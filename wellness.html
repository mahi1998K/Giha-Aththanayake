What is Gihan Aththanayake Vitality?

A Wellness Program That Rewards You

AIA Vitality is a science-backed wellness program that helps you understand your health status, motivates you to improve your health, and rewards you for making healthier choices.

The healthier you live, the more you save. It's that simple. Track your activity, get personalized health insights, and earn rewards from leading brands and discounts on your premiums.

How It Works

Three Simple Steps

Getting started with AIA Vitality is easy. Here's how you can begin your wellness journey.

1
📋

Know Your Health

Take the Vitality Health Review — an online health assessment that evaluates your lifestyle, diet, exercise habits, and medical history to give you a personalized health score.

2
🏃

Improve Your Health

Earn Vitality points by exercising, eating healthy, getting screenings, and practicing mindfulness. Connect your fitness device to track activity automatically.

3
🎁

Get Rewarded

The more points you earn, the higher your Vitality status — and the bigger your rewards! Enjoy premium discounts, partner rewards, and exclusive experiences.

Earn Points

Ways to Earn Vitality Points

There are many ways to rack up points and climb your Vitality status. Here are just a few.

🏋️

Exercise & Fitness

Walk, run, cycle, swim, or hit the gym. Track with Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, or Samsung devices. Earn up to 100 points daily.

🥗

Healthy Eating

Log your meals and make healthier food choices. Purchase healthy food from partner stores and earn bonus points.

🩺

Health Screenings

Get your annual health check-up, blood tests, BMI measurement, and dental check-ups. Earn up to 5,000 points per screening.

🧘

Mindfulness

Practice meditation, deep breathing, and yoga. Use our partner apps like Calm and Headspace to earn mindfulness points.

📚

Health Knowledge

Complete health quizzes, watch educational content, and read wellness articles on the Vitality app to earn knowledge points.

🏆

Challenges & Events

Participate in Gihan Aththanayake wellness events, charity runs, step challenges, and community fitness activities for bonus points.
