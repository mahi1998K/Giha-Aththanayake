❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about life insurance and our policies.

What is life insurance and why do I need it?

+
Life insurance provides financial protection for your loved ones in the event of your passing. It ensures that your family can maintain their standard of living, pay off debts, fund children's education, and cover daily expenses even when you're no longer there to provide.

How much life insurance coverage do I need?

+
A good rule of thumb is 10-15 times your annual income. However, the ideal coverage depends on your family's specific needs, debts, future goals, and lifestyle. Our financial advisors can help you determine the right amount during a free consultation.

Can I get health and life insurance together?

+
Yes! Many of our plans offer integrated health and life coverage. Plans like AIA Suwa Diriya combine critical illness coverage with life protection. You can also add health riders to any of our base life insurance plans for comprehensive coverage.
