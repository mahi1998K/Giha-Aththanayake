Our Story

Born From a Vision to Protect

Gihan Aththanayake is a professional Life Insurance Advisor in Sri Lanka dedicated to helping individuals, families, and professionals protect their financial future through reliable insurance planning.

With years of experience in the insurance industry and currently representing AIA Insurance, Gihan specializes in life insurance, health protection, income protection, and long-term financial security planning. His mission is to help clients make informed financial decisions that safeguard their loved ones and their future.

Gihan believes that life insurance is not just a policy — it is a financial safety net that protects families from unexpected risks. By understanding each client’s lifestyle, goals, and responsibilities, he provides customized insurance solutions designed to ensure financial stability and peace of mind.

Over the years, Gihan has built a strong reputation as a trusted Life Insurance Advisor in Sri Lanka by successfully serving 100+ satisfied clients and assisting with 30+ insurance claim settlements. His commitment to transparency, ethical advice, and long-term client relationships has made him a reliable partner for individuals seeking financial protection.

Before entering the insurance industry, Gihan gained professional experience in the construction and engineering sector, which strengthened his analytical thinking and problem-solving skills. This background helps him approach financial protection planning with a structured and strategic mindset.

Today, Gihan continues to empower Sri Lankan families and professionals by educating them on the importance of life insurance, health protection, and financial planning. His goal is to build a future where more people are financially protected and prepared for life’s uncertainties.
